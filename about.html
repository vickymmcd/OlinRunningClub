About the Olin Running Club!

The Olin Running Club is open to all students no matter your running experience or background. Members are welcome to come whenever they can and we will do our best to help all our runners achieve their goals. We will also give our runners the opportunity to compete in races of various distances and levels of competition, from casual fun races to team races sanctioned by the National Intercollegiate Running Club Association (NIRCA).
