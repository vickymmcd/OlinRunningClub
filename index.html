Welcome to the home of the Olin College Running Club!

The Olin College Running Club is open to all students interested in running in any way. Feel free to show up only occasionally to go on runs with friends or to show up to every practice to improve and compete in road races and NIRCA sanctioned cross country and track meets. We welcome all levels and abilities. We hope you will join us!
